Powering the Future: Our Vision for Energy

We invest in the companies shaping the next generation of nuclear energy—backing innovators in small modular reactors (SMRs), advanced fuels, and critical technologies that power this transformation. As global energy needs accelerate, fueled by electrification, data growth, and EV adoption, we see nuclear as a cornerstone for delivering reliable, clean energy at scale.

While renewables remain a vital piece of the energy puzzle, their intermittent nature makes it clear that the world also needs stable, around-the-clock power solutions. With superior safety features, flexible deployment, and growing policy momentum, modern nuclear energy offers a clear path forward.

Our mission is to support the leaders building the energy infrastructure of the future—companies driving energy security, deep decarbonization, and sustainable economic growth.

Our Approach

Our approach centers on investing in breakthrough technologies that address some of the world’s most pressing challenges. We focus on ideas with the power to transform industries, strengthen communities, and fuel long-term growth.

We target sectors where innovation aligns with unstoppable global trends—energy, advanced manufacturing, automation, and AI—where the right solutions aren’t optional, they’re essential.

But we don’t stop at providing capital. We bring strategic insight, operational expertise, and long-term partnership, working alongside visionary teams to help their ideas scale and succeed. Our goal is to back technologies that not only generate strong returns but also leave a lasting mark on the world.
