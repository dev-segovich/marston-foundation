About GME Alliance

GME Alliance is a family office committed to building a safer, more resilient, and better-connected future by investing in overlooked sectors essential to modern life. We specialize in identifying high-growth opportunities across infrastructure, workforce housing, energy, mobility, and safety, with a focus on businesses and projects that improve how people live, move, and work.

Our investment strategy spans five core sectors:

  • Construction Safety & Industrial Innovation
  • Workforce & Market-Rate Housing Development
  • Telecom Infrastructure & Cell Towers
  • Recreational Vehicle (RV) Parts & Manufacturing
  • Clean & Nuclear Energy (including Small Modular Reactors)



We partner with founders, patent holders, and operators whose products and platforms solve real-world problems at scale. Whether backing a safety equipment company, entitling land for multifamily housing, or supporting the buildout of U.S. telecom networks, our focus is always on impact, defensibility, and long-term value creation.

Headquartered in the U.S., we source opportunities globally with a strong presence in Latin America and a deep understanding of how capital, regulation, and innovation intersect in today’s dynamic markets.
