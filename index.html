Empowering the Future of Skilled Trades and Communities

The Marston Foundation exists to close America’s most critical labor gaps and help communities unlock the full value of their assets—through two mission tracks:

1. Faith-Based Community Development
We work alongside churches and religious institutions to help them activate underutilized land for mission-aligned projects. These include affordable housing, education centers, and services that meet local needs—while creating new revenue to replace declining donations and sustain their ministries long-term.

2. Workforce Development in Skilled Trades (Coming Soon)
We fund and coordinate training opportunities embedded in real-world construction and nuclear energy projects. Whether it’s a housing development in Florida or a power facility in Missouri, our programs connect aspiring tradespeople with hands-on experience, mentorship, and a sustainable career path.
