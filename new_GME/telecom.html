Connecting the World: Our Vision for Telecom Infrastructure

We invest in the companies building the backbone of modern connectivity—those that secure land, construct cell towers, and lease them to the carriers powering our digital lives. With demand for mobile data surging and 5G transforming how we work, learn, and communicate, we see telecom infrastructure as a critical pillar of economic progress.

As carriers shift from owning towers to leasing them, a new generation of companies is stepping in to meet that need—developing high-performance networks in both urban and rural areas. These towers don’t just serve devices; they enable digital equity, support business growth, and bring entire communities online.

Our mission is to support the builders of this digital foundation—companies delivering reliable coverage, scalable platforms, and long-term value in the U.S., Latin America, and beyond.

Our Approach

We back infrastructure platforms that convert land into long-term cash-flowing assets—one tower at a time. Our strategy focuses on companies that control the full cycle: securing leases, building vertical assets, hosting multiple tenants, and selling stabilized portfolios to institutional buyers.

We target telecom infrastructure because it intersects two unstoppable forces—data demand and digital inclusion. These aren’t short-term trends; they’re structural transformations reshaping the global economy.

Beyond capital, we provide strategic guidance and operational insight, ensuring each asset delivers both economic and social return. Our goal is to partner with companies that bridge connectivity gaps while generating resilient, inflation-linked income for decades to come.
